# Week Seven: Electronic Government Procurement (eGP) Database Management and ICT Support

## Overview

The seventh week of my industrial training focused on database management, information system analysis, and ICT infrastructure support at Mukono District Local Government.

The major technical activity involved working with the Electronic Government Procurement (eGP) database system, which provided practical exposure to how large-scale relational databases are structured, managed, and used to support government procurement processes.

Additional activities involved computer system maintenance, software deployment, hardware troubleshooting, and network device configuration.

---

## Activities Completed

### Electronic Government Procurement (eGP) Database Analysis

- Studied the structure and organization of the eGP relational database system.
- Analyzed relationships between different database entities.
- Explored how procurement information is stored, managed, and retrieved.
- Examined database components including:
  - Supplier registration
  - Procurement plans
  - Tender management
  - Bid submissions
  - Bid evaluations
  - Contract management
  - Procurement reports

### Database Management Activities

- Reviewed supplier information management processes.
- Studied how procurement records are linked across different database tables.
- Explored how database systems support transparency, accountability, and reporting.
- Observed data retrieval and reporting processes within the procurement system.

### ICT Technical Support Activities

- Updated BIOS and firmware on selected computer systems.
- Installed and configured Microsoft Office applications.
- Installed and configured wireless network drivers on HP computers.
- Diagnosed and troubleshot biometric attendance system faults.
- Assisted in wireless router configuration and network setup.
- Verified system functionality after maintenance activities.
